Privacy Policy for NewKhata

Last updated: February 12, 2026

Introduction

NewKhata ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address when you sign in with Google or other authentication providers
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and device identifiers for backup synchronization across devices

Financial Data

  • Transaction Records: Records of financial transactions you enter
  • Person/Contact Details: Names and contact information you enter for transaction parties
  • Financial Calculations: Balances and other calculated financial information

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

  • Providing Core Functionality: Managing your transaction records and calculations
  • Backup & Synchronization: Backing up your data to our secure servers (only when you explicitly request backup)
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing app usage patterns to improve functionality (anonymized)
  • Support: Addressing your questions and troubleshooting issues

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage

  • Your transaction data is primarily stored locally on your device using secure storage methods
  • Local data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption

Cloud Backup

  • Your data is backed up to Firebase only when you initiate a backup
  • All cloud data is encrypted during transmission and at rest
  • We maintain 60 days of transaction history in backups

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With service providers who assist us in operating our service (like Firebase)

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data and account
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of backups

Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored until you delete it from your device
  • Backup data: We retain your most recent backup data until you delete your account

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
